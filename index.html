What is XR Meditation?

XR Meditation is a virtual reality mindfulness solution designed to bring the proven benefits of nature-based meditation into the workplace. Users can escape the office and immerse themselves in tranquil Finnish and Japanese forest environments, experiencing the stress-relieving effects of shinrin-yoku (forest bathing) without leaving their desk.

Immersive VR Environment

Finnish and Japanese forest settings designed for mindfulness and relaxation.

Real-Time Biofeedback

Integration with Muse EEG headbands to track stress levels and adjust the environment.

Progressive Experience

Unlock new environments and features as meditation milestones are achieved.

Companion App

Track progress, receive reminders, and view biofeedback insights on your smartphone.

Research Focus

Researching the Impact of Virtual Nature on Well-Being

XR Meditation is more than an app—it's part of an ongoing research project exploring how virtual environments can support workplace well-being. Our studies focus on whether short, VR-based mindfulness sessions can deliver the same benefits as real-world nature exposure, including stress reduction, mental clarity, and resilience.

EEG-Driven Biofeedback

Using Muse EEG headbands to measure stress levels and track relaxation progress.

Nature and Well-Being

Exploring how immersive nature environments affect cognitive and emotional well-being.

Mindfulness at Work

Research led by Monika’s organizational studies, examining how VR can help employees disconnect from work stress and recharge effectively.
